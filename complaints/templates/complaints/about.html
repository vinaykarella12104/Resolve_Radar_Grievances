About the Grievance Management System

Welcome to the Grievance Management System, an efficient platform designed to help users lodge grievances with ease and transparency. Our system ensures that all grievances are tracked, managed, and resolved in a timely manner.

We understand the importance of addressing issues promptly, and our goal is to provide a seamless experience for users to report any problems they encounter. Whether you are a student, staff member, or administrator, our system is equipped to handle all your needs.

Information about the Status of the Grievances

Filed - The grievance is successfully sent to the admin.

In Progress - The grievance has been accepted and is in progress.

Resolved - The grievance has been successfully resolved.

Rejected - The grievance has been rejected.

Thank you for using the Grievance Management System. We are committed to continually improving our services to better serve your needs.
